Chocolate lava cookies

What it's like

A yummy chocolate cookie, filled with chocolate. I don't know what they actually taste like. Because I haven't tried them.

Ingredients

  • 6 ounces of chocolate
  • 6 ounces of butter
  • 5 chicken eggs room temp
  • half a cup of sugar
  • pinch of salt
  • 6 tablespoons of flour

Steps

  1. Butter 8 ramekins and sprinkle with flour
  2. Melt
  3. Melt chocolate and Butter on low until smooth
  4. Beat eggs with sugar & salt for 6-8 minutes or until thick and creamy
  5. Slowly add flour 1 tbsp. at a time then slowly beat in choc sauce
  6. Pour into ramekins and place in preheated oven 350 degrees fahrenheit for 11-15 min.
  7. Place plate over ramekin and invert
  8. Sprinkle with powdered sugar and raspberries
