Artichoke Dip

What it's like

A Delicous, cheesy, and creamy dip that is very good with some crackers or corn chips.

Ingredients

  • One cup of shredded mozzarela
  • 400ml of artichoke hearts(cut fine)
  • One cup of grated parmesan
  • One cup of mayonaisse
  • Dash of garlic powder, parsley flakes

Steps

  1. Mix well
  2. Bake at 350 degrees fahrenheit for 30 minutes or until sheese is melted or golden brown around the edges
